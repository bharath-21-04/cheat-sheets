Meeting Controls

⌘⇧A / Alt+A           Mute/unmute audio
⌘⇧V / Alt+V           Start/stop video
⌘⇧S / Alt+S           Screen share
⌘⇧H / Alt+H           Show/hide chat
⌘U / Alt+U             Participants panel
⌘⇧R / Alt+R           Start/stop recording
⌘W / Alt+Q             End meeting (host)
⌘J / Alt+J             Join meeting

# Quick start
⌘⇧N                   New meeting
⌘⇧M                   Schedule meeting
🎥

Video & Audio

⌘⇧A / Alt+A           Mute audio
Space (hold)           Push-to-talk (muted)
⌘⇧V / Alt+V           Camera on/off
⌘⇧N / Alt+N           Camera switch
⌘⇧T                   Pause/resume share

Settings → Backgrounds & Effects
Blur, images, video backgrounds

# Audio settings
Click ^ on Mute → Audio Settings
Test speaker & microphone
Noise suppression: Auto/Low/Medium/High
